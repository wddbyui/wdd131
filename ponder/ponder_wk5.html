This demonstrates our ponder web page with different layouts according to the device size:

Modals

  1. Each image in this activity has a lower and higher resolution version. The lower resolution images are used in the gallery thumbnails and the higher resolution image is used in the modal.

    When the user clicks the thumbnail the same higher resolution image shows in the modal.

    The modal can be closed with the close button with the 'X' by clicking outside the modal image, or by pressing the 'Esc' key.
